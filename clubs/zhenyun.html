About the Club

Founded in March 2023, the Essence Practice Hub is a vibrant community dedicated to the daily practice of holistic well-being and personal growth. Our diverse range of activities includes:

  • 1-Minute English Presentations: Daily online submissions of 1-minute English presentations, with peer feedback provided to enhance communication skills.
  • Mindful Tea and Dietary Gatherings: Periodic in-person gatherings focused on mindful tea ceremonies and conscious eating practices.
  • Offline Traditional Chinese Exercises: Daily offline practice of traditional Chinese exercises, such as Cloud Hands, Wind Fire Wheels, and Ba Duan Jin, with daily check-ins for accountability.
  • Daily Stoic Reflections: Rotating hosts select questions from "The Daily Stoic" book, post them in the group, and members collaborate on shared online documents to provide answers and insights.
