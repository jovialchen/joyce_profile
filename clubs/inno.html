Innovation Team

About the Club

Our innovation team is a vibrant, employee-driven initiative within the company, dedicated to fostering a culture of creativity and ingenuity. We operate outside of regular work hours, with members contributing their time and expertise to drive innovation. We achieve this through a variety of initiatives, including hosting workshops on patent writing, organizing the annual 'Golden Idea' competition, and publishing our yearly technical journal. These activities provide a platform for members to explore their innovative potential and contribute to the advancement of knowledge, all while building professional development.

My Contributions

During my involvement with the team from 2013 to 2018, I played a pivotal role in the production of our technical journal, contributing my efforts outside of regular work hours. My responsibilities encompassed the entire publication process, including:

  • Soliciting submissions
  • Creating LaTeX templates
  • Providing video tutorials for template usage
  • Coordinating with designers for cover art
  • Managing the peer-review process
  • Overseeing typesetting, printing, and digital distribution
In 2013, I served as the primary formatting editor, and from 2014 to 2018, I took on end-to-end responsibility for the journal's production.
