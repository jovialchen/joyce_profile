About the Reading Club

Founded in June 2021, our company reading club provides a welcoming and enriching space for well-educated women engineers to connect, relax, and foster continuous growth. Meeting bi-weekly for one hour, both online and in-person, we delve into member-selected books chosen to inspire personal and professional development. Beyond book discussions, we also host diverse sharing sessions covering topics like psychology, effective note-taking, and even mentorship opportunities with young students. This initiative creates a vibrant community focused on learning and mutual support within the company.

My Contributions

As one of the founding members and a major organizer of this reading club, I have been instrumental in shaping its vision and ensuring its ongoing success. From the initial conceptualization and launch in June 2021, I have taken a leadership role in member recruitment, meeting coordination, book selection processes, and the implementation of our diverse sharing sessions.
